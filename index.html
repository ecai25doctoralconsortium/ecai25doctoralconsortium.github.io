Doctoral Consortium at ECAI 2025

The 28th European Conference on Artificial Intelligence (ECAI-2025) will be held in the beautiful city of Bologna during 25-30 October 2025. We invite PhD students working in the broad research areas covered by ECAI to participate in the Doctoral Consortium (DC) of ECAI-2025. The DC is an opportunity to meet and interact closely with established researchers in your field, as well as other PhD students. The DC also offers an opportunity to receive feedback on your work, and to get advice on managing your career.

Key dates:

  • Submission site opening: Monday, 9 June 2025
  • Submission deadline: Friday, 11 July 2025
  • Notification: Friday, 25 July 2025
  • Final version for DC website due: Thursday, 11 September 2025
  • Doctoral Consortium date: Saturday, 25 October 2025

All deadlines are at the end of the day specified, anywhere on Earth (UTC-12).
